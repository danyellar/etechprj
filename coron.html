Coron Island, Palawan

Coron is the name of an island off the coast of Busuanga as well as the name of the largest town in Busuanga. The people of Tagbanua live on Coron Island, which is a beautiful landscape and is a natural wonder, with towering limestone cliffs, sand beaches that are white, and seven mountain lakes. Forbes Travel magazine has ranked this island as one of the Top 10 best scuba diving spots in the world.

A secret lagoon with crystal blue-green seas, limestone formations, and white sand coastlines is also hidden behind majestic rock formations. This hidden beach is just wonderful! However, you'll have to work hard to get there, either by scaling cliffs or swimming through a sea tunnel. But it's completely worth it!

When to Visit

Visiting during the off-season will still be great and will help avoid the island being crowded even if Coron is not that popular unlike other beaches.

How to Get There

To get there, you need to take a flight into Busuanga and then book a van to Coron (this takes approximately 45 minutes). Take note that no one is permitted to stay on the island overnight, although accommodations on housing are available in Coron, Busuanga.

What To Do

You can spend your time going scuba diving! Coron is one of the best spots in the world for scuba diving.

What it is Known For

Beautiful limestone cliffs and shipwreck diving spots are popular on this island.
