Palaui Island, Cagayan Valley

CNN listed Palaui Island at tenth place of their Top 100 Best Beaches throughout the world, characterizing this island as simple and an incredible beauty and elegance wherein nature emerges into foliage and wildlife, columnar basalt, dazzling white sands and plenteous coastal ecosystems.

This island is a true paradise, no hotels as well as other lavish goods are obtained and seen here. You have to seek a guest house or you can camp on the beach if you plan on staying (although camping is now not allowed near the lighthouse).

When to Visit

At the same time as Coron.

How to Get There

Head straight towards Sta. Ana, Cagayan from Manila, either taking a bus or a car. You may fly to Tugeugarao or you may take a bus for the remaining distance. When you’ve arrived in Sta. Ana, you should go to San Vicente, then rent a boat to Palaui.

What To Do

You may hike, beach bum, or take a tour of the lighthouse.

What it is Known For

A historic lighthouse and unspoiled beaches In 2013, a season of the popular American television show Survivor was filmed there.
